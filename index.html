CRM System For
Legal Entities

CRM for legal entities - the system is designed to develop a customer
relationship management system for legal entities and individual
entrepreneurs. It streamlines management processes, enabling better
resource allocation for business development. With faster access to
comprehensive customer data, personalized offerings

CHALLENGE

Creating effective sales and control tools
through the CRM system for legal entities.The
organization aimed to enhance sales and
control, reduce errors, improve management
efficiency, and achieve faster access to quality
customer data. Additionally, the system
targeted the conversion of inactive clients by
providing personalized recommendations.

SOLUTION

With the help of CRM, managers make fewer
mistakes, which means they sell more
frequently and in larger quantities. It also
becomes easier for the company's
management to oversee operations, as they
spend less time on control and gain more
resources to develop the business.

FUNCTIONALITY

  • Viewing a legal entity card
  • Launching processes from a profile
  • Scale for measuring the fullness of the profile
    with data
  • Customer rating
  • Customer's grocery basket
  • Bottom-up NPS by client

TECHNOLOGY STACK

  • Java
  • Spring framework
  • Postgresql
  • Elasticsearch
  • Redis
  • Kafka
  • Hibernate
  • Angular

SYSTEM SPECIFICATION

The CRM system for legal entities is implemented on the Camunda BPM platform
